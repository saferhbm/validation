The validation catalogue for the SAFER HBM highlights:

- **Submodel Validation**: Ensuring accuracy in representing body regions.
- **Full body model validation**: Validation across frontal, side, and rear impacts.
- **Acceptance Criteria**: Establishment of clear model acceptance standards.
- **Injury Evaluation**: Assessing model-predicted injuries against test injury data.
