Front, Side and Oblique#

  • Setup :Cervical spine FSU

    • MAT_RIGID sled with material properties of steel.

    • MAT_RIGID elliptical ring with weight \(5 kg\) to match the pretension forces applied during tests. Elliptical ring with major diameter=\(311 mm\) and minor diameter=\(172 mm\)

    • MAT_ELASTIC potting material with \(E=03.42Gpa\), \(density=1.14 g/cm^3\) and \(pr=0.33\)

  • Contacts : *CONTACT_AUTOMATIC_SURFACE_TO_SURFACE with \(fs=0.3 , vdc=20, soft=2\) between the HBM and the elliptical ring

Positioning and Pre-simulation

  • Initial position - specimen:

    • Horizontal Distance from head CoG to center axis of the potting is defined as \(81 \pm 34 mm\)

    • Vertical Distance from head CoG to center axis of the potting is defined as \(229 \pm 21 mm\)

    • angle of the T1 superior endplate maintained during porring around \(24 \pm 5 degree\)

  • Initial position - Potting w.r.t the elliptical ring:

    • potting center to elleptical ring center is \(28 mm\) apart about the minor axis of the elliptical ring.

    • Vertical Distance from head CoG to center axis of the potting is defined as \(229 \pm 21 mm\)

  • Pre-Simulation:

    • The specimen is let to settle under gravity for the neck muscles to get pretensed for \(300 ms\) with defined GLOBAL_DAMPING of magnitude \(0.057\)

  • Constraints

    • *CONSTRAINT_SHELL_IN_SOLID_PENALTY used for the potting.

    • *BOUNDARY_PRESCRIBED_MOTION_RIGID to set the fix the Dof of the elleptical ring and seld other than the loading direction.

Important

The elliptical ring is free to move vertically during presimulation.

  • Loads :

    • *BOUNDARY_PRESCRIBED_MOTION_RIGID define constant acceleration pulse in frontal, side and oblique angles to the sled and ellecptical ring.

Twist#

  • Contacts :

    • *CONTACT_AUTOMATIC_SURFACE_TO_SURFACE with \(fs=0.3 , vdc=20, soft=2\) between the HBM and the elliptical ring.

    • *CONTACT_AUTOMATIC_SURFACE_TO_SURFACE with \(fs=0.7 , vdc=20, soft=1\) between the HBM and clamps.

Pre-simulation

  • Initial positioning and appying muscle pretension followed like the above setup.

  • \(50 ms\) pre run to clamp the head using two rigid rectangular surface to fix the head in all directions.

Constraints same as the front,side and oblique is used but the sled is loaded in z-rotation using velocity curve provided in the litrature.
